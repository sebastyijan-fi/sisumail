Sisumail receive-only sovereign mail
Not an outbound email platform

Your inbox. Your keys. Your rules.

Sisumail is infrastructure for receiving email under an identity you control. It does one job: get messages to your device without turning you into a product.

Install CLI (Linux/macOS) Discovery JSON Source

How It Feels

You get a Sisumail identity and a calm local inbox. You can hand out unlimited aliases so you instantly know who leaked your address.

Your address:
inbox@niklas.sisumail.fi

Aliases (same inbox):
steam@niklas.sisumail.fi
bank@niklas.sisumail.fi
random-thing@niklas.sisumail.fi

Outbound email sending is intentionally not part of Sisumail. Keep your existing provider for sending. Use Sisumail to receive with sovereignty.

Get Started

Install and run:

curl -fsSL https://sisumail.fi/install.sh | bash

sisumail -init
sisumail

open http://127.0.0.1:3490/app

Desktop app (Tauri) is coming. For now, the local web inbox runs on your machine and stays local-only.
